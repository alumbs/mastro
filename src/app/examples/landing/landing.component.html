Who we are?

The mission of MASTRO (Manitoba Association of Short Term Rental Owners Inc) is to advance the interests of the vibrant short-term rental industry of Manitoba through advocacy, education, and sharing information. We expect our members to follow the law; to behave as good neighbors; and to welcome all guests regardless of race, color, sex, religion, familial status, sexual orientation, or national origin.

Friends of MASTRO

We are thankful for the businesses, property management firms, and others who stand with us in our advocacy and educational efforts as Friends of MASTRO. Their partnership enables us to continue our advocacy of property owner rights, enforcement of reasonable regulations, and responsible conduct within our neighborhoods.
