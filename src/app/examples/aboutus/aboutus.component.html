About Us

Who we are

MASTRO IS THE WINNIPEG AREA SHORT TERM RENTAL ASSOCIATION.

Our mission is to advance the interests of the vibrant short-term rental industry of Winnipeg and Manitoba through advocacy and education.


MASTRO members and friends are comprised of homeowners, concerned citizens, and businesses who support the short term rental community and seek to advocate for fair homesharing in the Manitoba area.


We expect our members to follow the law; to behave as good neighbors; and to welcome all guests regardless of race, color, sex, religion, familial status, sexual orientation, or national origin.

WE BELIEVE THAT RESPONSIBLE HOMESHARING BENEFITS NOT ONLY OURSELVES BUT ALSO OUR CITY AND OUR GUESTS.

How You Can Help

MASTRO is a not-for-profit 501(C)(6) business league organization run by volunteers. Dues and donations go largely toward funding the land-use attorney and lobbyist whom we have hired to provide advocacy, representation, and legal advice/interpretation on behalf of MASTRO and our members. Dues and donations are tax deductible.
